WESTERN SMART WALL PUTTY


Western Smart Wallputty is a white cement based Putty specially developed as a finish coat to cover unevenness and pinholes in plastered surfaces to make the walls/ceilings ready for painting. It is suitable for application on all internal surfaces & once set, it provides High Quality, durable & smooth surfaces for application of decorative finishes.

Features and Benefits


  • Provided smooth and uniform finish, thus increases coverage of paint
  • Excellent bonding to the base plaster / concrete resulting into reduction in flaking
  • High Tensile, Compressive & flexural strength
  • Reduces the cracks
  • Controlled water vapour permeability
  • Resists growth of algae and fungi on wall
