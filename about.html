About Us

Sai India Gypsum was Incorporated in the Year 2002 to cater to the Demands of Gypsum Powder Industry, it is part of diverse Sai Group.

Beginning with Manufacturing of Gypsum Powder from our Plant in Bikaner, Rajasthan, the Company has Diversified into Manufacturing and Distribution of high performance building materials, Providing Innovative Solutions to Challenges of growth, energy-efficiency and environmental protection.

Our Gypsum Based Products are Natural, recyclable, sustainable and non-hazardous.

Vision


Metamorphosis into a preferred choice for interior construction products in the construction industry by providing high-quality and cost-effective products, systems and solutions. We envision to build the best product, cause no unnecessary harm, use business to inspire and implement solutions.
