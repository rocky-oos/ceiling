Installation


  • Determine the exact location on the ceiling where the trap door needs to be installed
  • Once the periphery section is installed measure the distance Y and ensure the correctness in terms of size and right angle (perpendicularity)
  • Install Western Trap Door, by screw fixing it to the ceiling section
  • Install the (surrounding) ceiling gypsum board
  • Perform the jointing and finishing at the board joints as per the recommended Western Standards
  • Apply paint and other finishes
